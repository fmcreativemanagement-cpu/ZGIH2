Philippine Law

Are you required to
comply with EPR?

The Extended Producer Responsibility Act of 2022 (Republic Act 11898) requires plastic manufacturers, brand owners, importers, and FMCG companies to recover, recycle, and manage their plastic waste — targeting plastic-neutrality within 10 years.

📋 RA 11898 — EPR Act of 2022

If your company produces, imports, or sells products using plastic packaging, you may be required to register with the DENR and submit an annual EPR plan. ZGIH can be your official recovery partner.
