The Process

How a ZGIH CSR program works

From first conversation to final impact report — here's how we build your program together.

💬

Discovery Call

We discuss your sustainability goals, budget, and design a program that fits your company.

📋

Program Design

ZGIH prepares a customized proposal with targets, timelines, and reporting structure.

🚀

Implementation

Our team rolls out collection, training, education, or restoration programs in the community.
