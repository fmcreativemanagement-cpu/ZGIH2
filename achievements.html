Our Journey

ZGIH Milestones

From a small community initiative to a recognized NGO — here's how ZGIH has grown since its founding.

2019

ZGIH Founded in Zamboanga City

A small group of environmental advocates established ZGIH with a focus on solid waste reduction and community awareness in two pilot barangays.

2020

First Green Livelihood Training

Launched the inaugural upcycling skills training for 20 women in Barangay Canelar, producing the first batch of recycled products for local sale.

2021

Eco-Education Enters Public Schools

Partnered with DepEd to pilot the Solid Waste Education module in three public schools, reaching 450 students in Grades 7 and 8.

2022

Coastal Conservation Program Launched

Began systematic mangrove restoration and marine clean-up operations in coordination with local fisherfolk organizations and BFAR.

2023

5-Ton Waste Diversion Milestone

ZGIH reached a cumulative 5 tons of solid waste diverted from landfills, earning the Zero-Waste Champion recognition from Region IX.

2024

MOU with Western Mindanao State University

Formalized a research partnership for joint solid waste characterization studies and green innovation pilots across the Peninsula.

2025

Green Store Launched Online

Opened the ZGIH Green Store to expand market access for community artisans, connecting upcycled products with buyers across the Philippines.

2026

National Best NGO Environmental Program Award

Received DENR Region IX's top recognition for ZGIH's integrated approach to coastal conservation, waste management, and green livelihoods.
