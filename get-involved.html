Join the Movement

How would you like to contribute?

Choose the path that fits you best. Every action — big or small — helps build a greener, more sustainable Zamboanga City.

Volunteer

Give your time and skills to support ZGIH's environmental programs across Zamboanga City.

  • Community clean-up drives
  • Eco-crafting workshops
  • Environmental campaigns
  • Education & outreach
Volunteers are the heart of ZGIH. No experience required — just passion for the environment. We welcome students, professionals, retirees, and community leaders. Flexible schedules available on weekdays and weekends.

Organization Member

Join as an official ZGIH member and become part of the community driving sustainable change.

  • Free eco-crafting training
  • Livelihood program access
  • Market access for products
  • Voting rights & priority
Membership is open to Filipino citizens residing in Zamboanga City. Registration fee is Php 50.00 with annual dues of Php 100.00 and monthly dues of Php 20.00. All fees are non-refundable.
Apply for Membership

Partner with Us

Collaborate with ZGIH as an organization, business, or institution to amplify environmental impact.

  • Government agencies
  • NGOs & civil society
  • Academic institutions
  • Businesses & corporations
ZGIH actively seeks partnerships with government agencies (LGU, DOLE, DENR), NGOs, schools, and businesses. We offer co-branded programs, joint events, and shared impact documentation for all partners.

CSR Services

Structured corporate social responsibility packages for businesses making a meaningful environmental impact.

  • Bronze, Silver & Gold packages
  • Eco-crafting workshops
  • Community clean-up drives
  • Impact reports & documentation
Our CSR packages are designed for businesses that want to demonstrate environmental responsibility. Each package includes planning, implementation, documentation, and a final impact report you can use for your sustainability disclosures.

  • Eco-crafting materials

  • Livelihood support
