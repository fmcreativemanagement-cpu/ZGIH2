Who We Are

Innovation rooted in community

ZGIH brings together crafters, farmers, low-income communities, women, out-of-school youth, solo parents, and local partners to foster green innovation, sustainable livelihoods, and environmental conservation.

Our Vision

A thriving and self-sustaining Zamboanga City where communities actively participate in green innovation, environmental stewardship, and sustainable economic development.
