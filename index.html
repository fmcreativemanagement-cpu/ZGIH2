Vision

A thriving and self-sustaining Zamboanga City where communities actively participate in green innovation, environmental stewardship, and sustainable economic development.

Mission

To empower communities in Zamboanga City by providing training in eco-friendly crafts, advocating for environmental sustainability, and promoting innovative recycled products that support both livelihoods and conservation.

Strategy

We work with communities, government agencies, NGOs, businesses, and academic institutions through eco-crafting workshops, sustainable agriculture programs, green entrepreneurship training, environmental awareness campaigns, and community livelihood support for women, youth, and solo parents.
