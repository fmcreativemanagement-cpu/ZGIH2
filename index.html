Who We Are

Innovation rooted
in community

ZGIH brings together local communities, students, entrepreneurs, and partners to co-create solutions for environmental challenges — from waste reduction to green livelihoods in Zamboanga City.

🌿

Environmental Stewardship

Protecting Zamboanga's extraordinary land and sea for the generations who will inherit them.

💡

Grassroots Innovation

Developing local, practical solutions shaped by people who know their communities best.

🤝

Inclusive Partnership

Collaborating with schools, government, businesses, and civil society across the Peninsula.
