MEMBERSHIP TERMS

  1. Qualification of Members. Membership shall be open only to a Filipino Citizen who has resided at least six (6) months continuously in Zamboanga City, of legal age, actively engaged in farming, fishing, home crafts, agricultural, self-employed and/or with employee-employer relationship.
  2. Application for Membership. The applicant must make her application for membership in writing a form provided by the association. It shall be the duty and responsibility of the officers to approve or disapprove the application.
  3. Resignation. A member may resign by tendering a letter for resignation 10 days before the actual date of her resignation so that the officers will have enough time to assess the accountabilities of said member.
  4. Termination. Any member who fails to attend three (3) consecutive organization meetings or violates major policy may be fined, suspended or terminated from membership.
  5. Death. The death of a member shall automatically terminate her membership. The legitimate heirs shall have the same rights of the equities of the deceased.

ASSOCIATION FEES, DUES AND OTHERS

  • Membership Fees. Upon admission, each member is required to pay a membership fee of Php 50.00.
  • Annual Dues. Annual due of Php 100.00 is required for every member to be paid on the 30th day of April every year.
  • Monthly Dues. A monthly due of Php 20.00 is required. Accumulated amount for the year in total of Php 240.00 to be paid on the 30th day of June.
  • Contribution. The association may gather funds through contributions or donations from both members and non-members, either in cash, labor, or in kind.
  • Savings Fund and Operation. The association shall establish a member-savings scheme. The accumulated fund shall be deposited in a chosen depositary bank.

Note: All collection fees from the members are non-refundable.
